ABOUT THE TERRACE

The Terrace is an artist-led, independent arts space and residency created to provide a focused environment for artists to reflect, create and grow. It offers respite from the everyday challenges of creative careers, supporting meaningful connections and artistic exploration.

Guided by the inclusive Sawazisha (Equalize) approach, The Terrace supports multidisciplinary artists through training, career development, and community-driven projects, including Sawazisha Sinema (Film) and Sawazisha Sekta (Music & Fashion).

Founded to decentralize the arts from major urban centers, The Terrace operates through three interconnected initiatives along Kilifi Creek:

  • The Terrace Art Space
  • The Terrace Residency (Professional & Emerging)
  • The Terrace Artists’ Exchange

The Terrace is an artist-led, independent arts space and residency, created to provide a focused environment for artists to reflect, create, and grow. It offers respite from the everyday challenges of creative and culture-making careers, supporting meaningful connections and artistic exploration.

Guided by the inclusive Sawazisha (Equalize) approach, The Terrace supports multidisciplinary artists through training, career development, and community-driven projects, including: Sawazisha Sinema (Film) and Sawazisha Sekta (Music & Fashion)

Founded to decentralize the arts from major urban centers, The Terrace operates through three interconnected initiatives along Kilifi Creek, aiming to foster cultural dialogue, skill-sharing, and economic opportunity:

•The Terrace Art Space
•The Terrace Residency
•The Terrace Artists Exchange

  • The Terrace Art Space

    The Terrace Art Space is a non-profit driven by the belief that creativity can shift mindsets, strengthen communities, and spark meaningful, lasting change.

    View More

  • The Terrace Residency

    The Terrace Residency is a multidisciplinary creative hub that offers focused opportunities for emerging and established artists to create, perform, and exhibit their work.

    View More

  • The Terrace Exchange

    The Exchange is a collaborative program that connects artists with the fishing communities around the Creek, creating a mutual exchange of skills, stories and resources.
